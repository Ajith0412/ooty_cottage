Cab detail

Discover the serene beauty of Ooty with our exclusive one-day cab tour! Nestled in the Nilgiri Hills, Ooty, also known as the "Queen of Hill Stations," offers a perfect escape into nature. Our comfortable and hassle-free cab service ensures you explore the best of Ooty's attractions with ease and comfort.

Itinerary Highlights

  • Botanical Gardens: Start your day surrounded by the vibrant greenery of the Government Botanical Garden. Explore the vast array of exotic plants and see the ancient 20-million-year-old fossil tree.

  • Doddabetta Peak: Ascend to the highest point in the Nilgiris, Doddabetta Peak, for jaw-dropping panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys.

  • Tea Garden & Tea Factory: Wander through the scenic tea gardens, where the hills are blanketed with lush green tea plants. Visit the Tea Factory and Museum to witness the traditional tea-making process and savor a cup of fresh Nilgiri tea.

  • Thread Garden: Be captivated by the extraordinary Thread Garden, an awe-inspiring display of handcrafted plants and flowers, all created using thread and no needles. This unique attraction is a tribute to the skill and patience of Ooty's artisans.

  • Ooty Lake & Boat House: Relax by the picturesque Ooty Lake, where you can take a leisurely boat ride while soaking in the surrounding natural beauty.

  • Eagle Dare Adventure: For thrill-seekers, Eagle Dare Adventure Park offers a rush of excitement with zip-lining, rope bridges, and more. Engage in heart-pounding activities against the scenic backdrop of Ooty.

  • Rose Garden: Take a stroll through the vibrant Rose Garden, home to thousands of blooming roses in a myriad of colors and varieties. The garden's enchanting fragrance and beauty will leave you spellbound.

  • Wax World Museum: End your day at the Wax World Museum, where lifelike wax figures of famous personalities bring Indian culture and history to life.

Why Choose Us?

Comfort & Exploration: Our cab service guarantees a smooth ride through Ooty’s finest spots, blending relaxation with adventure.

Customizable Tour: Whether you want to spend more time at the Thread Garden or embrace the thrill at Eagle Dare Adventure, we tailor the itinerary to your preferences.

Affordable Excellence: Enjoy a seamless and budget-friendly journey with transparent pricing and no hidden fees.
