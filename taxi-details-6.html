Cab detail

Escape to the tranquil charm of Coonoor with our one-day cab sightseeing tour! Nestled in the lush Nilgiri Hills, Coonoor is known for its tea plantations, stunning landscapes, and peaceful atmosphere. Let our reliable and comfortable cab service take you on an unforgettable journey through Coonoor’s most iconic spots.

Itinerary Highlights

  • Sim's Park: Begin your day at the beautiful Sim's Park, a botanical garden with over 1,000 plant species. Walk through well-manicured gardens, vibrant flower beds, and exotic trees while enjoying the cool mountain air.

  • Lamb's Rock: Take in the breathtaking views at Lamb's Rock, a viewpoint offering panoramic vistas of the lush valleys, tea estates, and forests below. This is a must-see spot for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

  • Dolphin's Nose: Head to Dolphin's Nose, one of the most famous viewpoints in Coonoor. The rock formation resembles a dolphin’s nose, and from here, you can witness the stunning landscape of misty mountains, deep ravines, and the cascading Catherine Falls.

  • Tea Gardens & Factory: Visit the renowned tea gardens of Coonoor, where the lush tea plantations roll across the hillsides. Take a tour of a local tea factory and witness the intricate process of tea production, from plucking to packaging. Don’t forget to enjoy a refreshing cup of freshly brewed Nilgiri tea!

  • Madras Regimental Centre (MRC): Explore the rich military heritage of India at the Madras Regimental Centre (MRC) in Wellington. Known for its historical significance, the MRC showcases the bravery and legacy of the Indian Army. Visit the museum, see the impressive military displays, and immerse yourself in the pride of the nation.

  • Highfield Tea Estate: End your day with a visit to Highfield Tea Estate, where you’ll learn more about tea cultivation in the Nilgiris. The serene surroundings and the aroma of fresh tea leaves make for a tranquil and enriching experience.

Why Choose Us?

Comfort & Exploration: Travel in style and comfort as we take you to Coonoor’s most captivating spots.

Customizable Tour: Want to spend more time exploring the tea plantations or soaking in the history at MRC? We customize the tour to suit your interests.

Affordable Excellence: With competitive pricing and no hidden costs, our tours offer great value for a memorable experience.
