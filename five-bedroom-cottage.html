Cottage detail

This five bedroom cottage, located 10 km from Ooty, offers a serene escape surrounded by expansive agricultural lands. Perched in an ideal top-view spot, the cottage provides breathtaking views of lush farmland. The peaceful setting is enhanced by the availability of a caretaker, ensuring comfort and assistance at any time.

For a truly relaxing experience, guests can enjoy an evening campfire or take an early morning stroll through the nearby plantation, perfect for unwinding and connecting with nature. Additionally, a unique local attraction is just 500 meters away—a carrot washing unit, offering an authentic glimpse into the region’s agricultural practices. This cottage is the perfect blend of comfort, relaxation, and rural charm.
