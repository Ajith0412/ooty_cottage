Why Choose Our Cab Service?

Comfortable Vehicles: Our fleet of well-maintained vehicles ensures a comfortable and pleasant journey, with spacious seating and modern amenities.

Professional Drivers: Our experienced drivers are well-versed in the Ooty to Mettupalayam route, ensuring a smooth and safe drive through winding mountain roads and bustling city streets.

Flexible Schedules: We offer flexible pickup times to suit your travel plans. Whether you need an early morning drop or a late afternoon ride, we’re here to accommodate your schedule.

Scenic Route: The journey takes you through some of the most beautiful landscapes, including lush tea gardens, rolling hills, and serene valleys. Enjoy the scenic views as you travel in comfort.

Hassle-Free Booking: Booking your cab with us is quick and easy. Just let us know your preferred date and time, and we’ll take care of the rest.

Competitive Pricing: We offer transparent and competitive pricing with no hidden charges, providing great value for a premium service.

Journey Details

Distance: Approximately 275 km

Duration: Around 6 to 7 hours, depending on traffic and road conditions.

Route: The most common route is via the NH181 and NH948, passing through picturesque towns and cities like Mysore.

Airport and Railway Station Drops: Need to catch a flight or train in Bengaluru? We offer timely drops to the airport or railway station, ensuring you reach your destination on time.

Customized Stops: We can accommodate stops along the way for meals, refreshments, or sightseeing, making your journey more enjoyable.
